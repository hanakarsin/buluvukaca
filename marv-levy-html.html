Marv Levy - Age, Family, Bio

Hall of Fame football coach in both the American and the Canadian leagues. He won four consecutive AFC Championships with the Buffalo Bills from 1990 to 1993.

Before Fame

After high school, he enlisted in the U.S. Marines Air Force to fight against Nazi Germany.

Trivia

After serving in WWII, he did not like people using war metaphors for sports.

Family Life

His father was a decorated World War I veteran.

Associated With

He selected the young and unknown Bill Walsh as his coaching assistant for the Cal Bears in 1960.
