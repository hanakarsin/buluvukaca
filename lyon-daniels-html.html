Lyon Daniels - Age, Family, Bio

Television and film actor who is best recognized for his role as Noodles in the Netflix film We Can Be Heroes. He also portrayed the character Efram on the Amazon series Patriot.

Before Fame

He began his career in the entertainment industry modeling for Sears, Kohls, Macy's, and Target when he was just 18 months old. 

Trivia

He has guest starred on the animated series Fundamentally Cynical. He has stated that he aspires to be a film director and producer. 

Family Life

He has two younger brothers named Rylan and Myles. His father is a musician, and his mother works in advertising. 

Associated With

He worked alongside director Robert Rodriguez on We Can Be Heroes.
