Kyle Chandler - Age, Family, Bio

Best known as Coach Eric Taylor on the television series Friday Night Lights; he also had starring roles in the series Early Edition and Bloodline. His film credits include Argo, Zero Dark Thirty, The Wolf of Wall Street, King Kong and Super 8. In 2019, he'd begin starring in Hulu's Catch-22.

Before Fame

He attended college at the University of Georgia before being scouted on one of ABC's talent searches.

Trivia

He won the 2011 Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series for his role on Friday Night Lights.

Family Life

He married Kathryn Chandler in 1995, with whom he has had two children. One of his daughters is actress Sydney Chandler.

Associated With

He played a supporting role alongside Ben Affleck in the Academy Award winning film Argo.
