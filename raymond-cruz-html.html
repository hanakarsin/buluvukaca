Raymond Cruz - Age, Family, Bio

Actor who is best known for playing military and law enforcement types on shows like The Closer, and in films like Under Siege. He is recognized for his recurring role as Tuco Salamanca on both Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul. 

Before Fame

He was raised in the rough streets of East Los Angeles and made his film debut in 1987's Maid to Order. 

Trivia

He owned a 1972 Moto Guzzi El Dorado police bike, which he worked on for 2 years. He has garnered more than 90 acting roles throughout this ongoing screen career. 

Family Life

He has said that he is extremely close to his mother. 

Associated With

He co-starred with Kyra Sedgwick on The Closer from 2005 to 2012.
