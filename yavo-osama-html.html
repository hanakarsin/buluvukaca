Yavo Osama - Age, Family, Bio

Southern-style hip hop artist who is best recognized for his singles "Shawn Kemp" and "Scoot Up". He released three full-length albums in 2020. 

Before Fame

He originally gained popularity by posting his music on his Big Yavo YouTube channel.

Trivia

He and rapper Luh Soldier have released the album Gorilla Warfare together. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Alabama. He has a son named Zayven who has appeared on his big_yavo Instagram account. 

Associated With

He has collaborated with rapper TLE Cinco on their single "41 Loads".
