Mickey Guyton - Age, Family, Bio

Capitol Records singer best known for the release of her single "Better Than You Left Me" in 2015. She debuted the single at the legendary theater the Grand Ole Opry.

Before Fame

At the age of five she began singing gospel in church. It wasn't until after LeAnn Rimes' performance of the national anthem at a Texas Rangers game that she found her calling in country music.

Trivia

She released her first EP Unbreakable in March 2014 followed by her self-titled EP in May 2015. Her debut studio album Remember Her Name was released in September 2021.

Family Life

She was born Candace Mycale Guyton to Michael Eugene Guyton and Phyllis Ann Roddy in Arlington, Texas. She has a younger sister. She and her husband, Grant Savoy, welcomed their first child in February 2021. 

Associated With

She has shared the stage with country superstar Brad Paisley.
