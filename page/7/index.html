Government Begs Npower Teachers To Resume

Aug 4, 2024 An appeal has gone out from the government of Osun State to former N-Power teachers to resume and prepare students for their forthcoming West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (WASSCE). He appealed to the teachers who were previously employed under the N-Teach category of the N-Power scheme and were handling the SS3 classes before their exit from the scheme to return to the schools and give revision classes to the students.
