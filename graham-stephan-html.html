Graham Stephan - Age, Family, Bio

Real estate agent and investor popular on YouTube where he has posted video content in order to share his successes, failures, and experiences in the real estate industry. He is also a car enthusiast and has shared automotive content with his more than 3.5 million subscribers.

Before Fame

He obtained his Real Estate license shortly after his 18th birthday.

Trivia

He owned six rental properties that he managed in his spare time throughout Los Angeles and San Bernardino County. He worked for the The Oppenheim Group.

Family Life

His girlfriend frequently appears in his Instagram photos. 

Associated With

Notable clients of his included Orlando Bloom, Suki Waterhouse and Chloë Grace Moretz.
