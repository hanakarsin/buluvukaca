Nadia Parkes - Age, Family, Bio

English actress who is best recognized for her roles in the television series The Spanish Princess and Doctor Who. She has also been cast in the television drama Domina. 

Before Fame

She has attended the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Arts. 

Trivia

She left school early to film The Spanish Princess. 

Family Life

She began dating actor Tom Holland in 2020, but the two later went their separate ways.

Associated With

She and actress Charlotte Hope both appeared on The Spanish Princess.
