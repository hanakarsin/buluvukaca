Johannah Duggar - Age, Family, Bio

Michelle and Jim-Bob Duggar's 16th overall child and 6th daughter who starred with the rest of the family on the hit TLC reality series 19 Kids and Counting.

Before Fame

She first appeared on the show when it was titled Raising 16 Children.

Trivia

She and all her siblings are home-schooled.

Family Life

Her immediate older sister is Joy-Anna who is almost 8 years older than her. She has three younger sisters Jennifer, Jordyn-Grace and Josie.

Associated With

She is close with her immediate older brother Jackson Duggar.
