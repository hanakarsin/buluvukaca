Mateo Agudelo - Age, Family, Bio

Comedy and POV video creator who has earned more than 850,000 fans for his matowelie TikTok account. He could be found on Snapchat by the username of Matskaterboy. He has also made numerous videos about relatable scenarios in romantic relationships. 

Before Fame

The oldest videos from his TikTok account dated back to the beginning of summer in 2019.

Trivia

He was a high school football player for Barron G. Collier High School. He mentioned in a 2021 video that he loves anime, weight lifting and gaming. 

Family Life

He is of Colombian descent and resided in Naples, Florida. He has a brother named Nico.

Associated With

He used the iLoveMemphis song "Hit the Quan" for multiple TikTok videos.
