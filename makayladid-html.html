makayladid - Age, Family, Bio

Singer, songwriter and content creator who became widely recognized for her makayladid TikTok account. Her clever comedy, music and dance footage has helped her acquire more than 1.1 million fans on the app. 

Before Fame

She graduated from a performing arts high school, then went on to earn her college degree in music production at LA Film School. In April 2017, she posted her premiere YouTube recording titled "High End Vs. Drugstore Makeup Tutorial." 

Trivia

She released her first EP Four Me in August 2019 and also dropped the official music video for her debut single "Millionaire" in October 2019. She posted a duet with Charlie Puth on her Instagram and the post has received over 7 thousand views. 

Family Life

She has featured her mother and other family members in her social media posts. 

Associated With

She went viral on Instagram with her cover of the Cardi B song "I Like It" and later made an official cover of the song on YouTube.
