Jack Doyle - Age, Family, Bio

American football player who has gained fame as a tight end for the Indianapolis Colts. He also had a brief stint in the Tennessee Titans.

Before Fame

He played high school football for the Cathedral Fighting Irish and earned All-State honors. He played college football for the Western Kentucky Hilltoppers.

Trivia

In 2018, he became the first Western Kentucky alumnus to be selected to the Pro Bowl.

Family Life

His full name is John Glenn Doyle. He has a wife named Casie and two sons named Ronan and William.

Associated With

He has played alongside Carson Wentz in the Indianapolis Colts.
