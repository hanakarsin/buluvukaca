ecampus UNIPORT |ecampus.uniport.edu.ng register and login

ecampus UNIPORT is a robust automated management solution utilized by the University of Port Harcourt. It is one of the most automated educational institution management solutions in Nigeria.

The ecampus portal enables you to access post UTME results, admission lists, apply for hostel allocation, pay your semester fees, check your semester examination results, and much more upon login. All the mentioned exercises can be done anywhere at your convenience.

ecampus.uniport info

The University of Port Harcourt has an online student portal that can be accessed by both formal and prospective students. The students can access the platform by simply logging in or creating accounts before logging in. In so doing, they will be able to perform certain academic functions online.

To use the ecampus portal, a student needs to understand the procedures required to access some of the services mentioned above. UNIPORT ecampus student portal can be accessed using your mobile phone or computer as long as you have access to the internet.

The online course application process

Application to study into the University of Port Harcourt can be made following the procedure below. Note that there are different application forms students have to fill, depending on the course of their choices.

  • Access the ecampus uniport page on your browser.
  • Click on ‘Home.’
  • Scroll down to ‘Online application forms.’
  • Click on ‘Go to online applications’. The next page will provide links to the specific courses you can apply for.
  • Click on the link of the course you want to apply for and proceed to fill the form.

    Accessing the admission list is a straightforward and fast exercise when you use the electronic platform. Do you want to view the university’s admission list using the institution’s portal? Check the procedure below.

  • Visit the website.
  • Click on ‘Admissions’.
  • Click on 'Search admission lists.'
  • Enter the required details.

    To search by surname/name
