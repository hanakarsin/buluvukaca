Full List Of Winners At City People Awards 2015

The City People Entertainment Awards held on Sunday, August 19, at Time Square event centre, Ikeja, Lagos, had in attendance several celebrities from the movie and music industries.

The winners of the night included Mavin Records, Don Jazzy, Mercy Aigbe, Reekado Banks, MI, Shina Peller and Odunlade Adekola, among others.
