She Is Not My Wife, She Is My Sister Kcee On The Juice

Limpopo crooner has been rumoured to be married for some time now, and on his recent interview with Toolz, he said that the woman in the picture is his sister.

Kcee went on Toolz show "Juice" and Toolz decided to bring up the issue asking him about Ijeoma Cynthia Okonkwo (nee Oduah).

Excerpts: Toolz: Do you know a lady called Ijeoma Okonkwo?

Kcee: Ijeoma is my elder sister. We are 7 in our family, Ijeoma is my elder sister.

Toolz: We heard you are married to this lady?

Kcee: Ijeoma is my elder sister... My immediate elder sister is Angela...

Toolz (shows him a picture from their rumoured 2010 wedding): Would your elder sister hold you like that? Kcee: Yes! That's my elder sister. No.. that's my younger sister, that's not Angela.
