I Still 'Speak' With My Dead Wife

The General Overseer of Trinity House Pastor Ituah Ighodalo has said that he still speaks to his wife, Ibidunni Ighodalo years after her death.

Recall that Ibidunni died of cardiac arrest at the young age of 39 in a hotel room in Port Harcourt where she went for an official assignment.

Speaking on their bond in Life, the clergyman stated that his connection with his wife is evergreen.

He said they still have discussion and he hears her speak.

He said, “We still converse, we talk, I hear her, she speaks to me”.

Speaking on their relationship, he said, “We had a good relationship, we understood one another, we loved one another, we were friends”.

Ituah also spoke on the crash of his former church, Christ church.

He said: “Quite frankly I genuinely love people, I love to see people succeed, I love to see people excel. I will go to the gutters to be with my people just to make sure everybody is okay.

“Christ Church was everything to me because I saw my vision, my dreams, my everything in Christ church. It was very heartbreaking for me”.
