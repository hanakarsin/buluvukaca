Arsenal, Watford, Others Know Opponent In Carabao Cup Second Round [Full Draw]

North London giants Arsenal will take oo West Brom in the second round of the Carabao Cup, Naija News reports.

Aston Villa will travel to Barrow, while Newcastle take on Burnley.

Leeds United will host League One side Crewe, while Everton have drawn Championship opposition in Huddersfield Town.

Premier League new boys Watford will tackle Crystal Palace, Southampton will head to League Two outfit Newport County, while Brentford welcome Forest Green Rovers.

Naija News reports that the games will be played during the week beginning Monday August 23.

EPL champions Manchester City, Manchester United, Liverpool, European champions Chelsea, Leicester, West Ham and Tottenham will all feature in the third-round draw.
