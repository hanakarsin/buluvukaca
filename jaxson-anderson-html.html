Jaxson Anderson - Age, Family, Bio

Popular social media personality who became best known for his jaxsonandersonn TikTok account by posting lip sync videos and often collaborating with other creators on duets. He has earned over 410,000 fans on the platform. 

Before Fame

His social media stardom started to come to fruition in the summer of 2015.  

Trivia

He was invited to be a part of the Impact Meet & Greet tour in 2016. He has over 120,000 followers on Instagram where he primarily posts selfies and other pictures of himself, occasionally alongside friends. He posted a TikTok video from the hospital when he got his deviated septum fixed in 2020. 

Family Life

He is from St. Petersburg, Florida. He has a sister.  

Associated With

He, Josh Levi, Zach Herron, Simon Britton, and many others were invited to part of the Impact Meet & Greet tour.
