Zach Aguilar - Age, Family, Bio

Voice actor who is best recognized for his roles as Koichi Hirose in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure and as Tanjiro in Demon Slayer the Movie: Mugen Train. He has voiced characters in several films, television shows, and video games throughout his career. 

Before Fame

He made his voice acting debut in 2004 on the video game World of Warcraft. 

Trivia

He voiced the character Byleth in the 2018 video game Super Smash Bros. Ultimate.

Family Life

He has a dog and a cat whom he sometimes features on his Instagram account.

Associated With

He has starred alongside Bryce Papenbrook in the series The Seven Deadly Sins.
