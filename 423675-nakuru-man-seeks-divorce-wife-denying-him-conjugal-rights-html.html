Nakuru: Man Seeks to Divorce Wife for Denying Him Conjugal Rights

  • A 30-year-old man said he wants to divorce his wife whom he wedded in 2016 due to lack of intimacy in the marriage
  • The man said the woman disclosed to him much later that she is only attracted to females
  • He told the court when he married the lady, they only got to consummate the union eight months after the wedding

A man in Nakuru has sought to divorce his wife over lack of intimacy in the marriage and the discovery that she is a lesbian.

The 30-year-old man identified as FJO said he met his lover also identified as PNM in the court papers, on Facebook in 2015 and in April 2016, the two would tie the knot for what FJO thought would be a lifetime of bliss.

However, the man's bubble would soon burst as he realised his new wife was not interested in consummating their union, the Standard reported.

In the court documents, the man told Nakuru principal magistrate Yvonne Khatambi, that PNM refused to have relations with him after the wedding. She only accepted to sleep with him eight months later.

The man, who said he shares a daughter with his wife, said their relationship deteriorated due to communication breakdown and lack of intimacy.

Attracted to females

The father of one said after some time, the woman disclosed to the man's sister and sister-in-law that she is only attracted to women.

"The respondent confided in my sister and my sister-in-law she was only attracted to females. It was this at this point she disclosed she is a lesbian," he said in his suit.

He noted he had sought help from family members and even professionals to make the marriage work but his efforts were futile.

FJO accused his wife of denying him conjugal rights without giving him satisfactory reasons on why they could not be intimate.

"It was after that (disclosing her sexuality to his sister and sister-in-law) when she told me that she has no interest in males and is a lesbian," he said.

The woman is yet to file a response for the suit. The magistrate will give her ruling on the divorce case on September 3.

Woman celebrates after divorcing husband

In a separate incident, TUKO.co.ke reported about a lady who celebrated after finally getting a divorce from her husband, noting she is very relieved.

Tracy Dimarco, from the US, shared selfies on Instagram showing her delight, alongside a lengthy message narrating reasons that led her to go the single mum route.

The mother of three never wished for her family to be separated, but about three years ago, things started to become different in her marriage.
