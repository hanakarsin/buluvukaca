Controversial video emerges showing Uhuru and Ruto giving KSh 70M in cash in a Harambee

- Uhuruto are known to contribute millions in harambees

- Yet another controversial video has emerged showing the two dishing out millions in cash

Deputy President William Ruto is well known for dishing out millions of shillings in harambees to churches and other institutions in the country.
