Is My Daddy The Man In Your Life?

Imade Adeleke, the seven years old daughter of Nigerian Afrobeat singer Davido, gave her mother a big shocker during an Instagram live session with her followers.

A few days ago, Davido and his baby mama Sophia Momodu celebrated their daughter’s birthday in the most beautiful way.

Davido also caused a stir online after gifting his daughter a toy which many claimed looked like Nigerian singer Burna Boy.

During an Instagram live session, a follower asked Sophia Momodu if she was single, noting that the man in her life is blessed because of her personality and she responded by saying she’s single.

The follower wrote: “Hi Sophie, Are you single? I know the man in your life is blessed and I love your personality.”

However, Imade gave Sophia a big surprise when she asked if her father, Davido was the man in her life.

Sophia Momodu surprised by the question burst into laughter and turned back to her followers reaffirming she’s single and has been single for a while now.

Imade said: “who is the man in your life, is it my daddy?”

The video generated mixed reactions online as some netizens claimed it was a planned question and Imade, a 7 year old child is too young to ask such a question.

Others also claimed that Sophia Momodu is excited about the question from her daughter and wants a possible relationship with Davido.
