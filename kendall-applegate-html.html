Kendall Applegate - Age, Family, Bio

During her childhood years, she rose to fame for her role as Penny Scavo in nearly fifty episodes of the ABC series Desperate Housewives.

Trivia

She appeared in a 2010 short film titled Ranger Rob: The Movie.

Family Life

She and her twin sister and fellow actress, McKenzie Applegate, spent their earliest days in Versailles, Kentucky.

Associated With
