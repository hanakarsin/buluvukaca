Black Market Dollar To Naira Exchange Rate December 7, 2021

Black market dollar to naira exchange rate December 7, 2021, can be obtained below.

Naija News reports that this page keeps our readers updated with the daily exchange rate of us dollar to naira in the parallel market popularly known as the black market which is operated by the Burea De Change (BDC) operators.

While dollar to naira exchange rate at the official market is important, we, however, note that this page rather focuses on the dollar to naira today black market rate.

How much is dollar to naira today in black market?

This page concentrates on the black market dollar to naira exchange rate and we know our readers will love to know how much is dollar to naira today in black market, hence, we have sourced the figures from various Bureau De Change (BDC) markets in Lagos, Nigeria’s commercial city.

Naija News reports that the local currency opened at N565 per $1 at the parallel market otherwise known as the black market, today, Tuesday, 7 December 2021 in Lagos Nigeria after it closed at N565.00 on Monday, December 6. This represents a change of N0 from the previous day.

I want to sell dollar and I want to buy dollar: I want to change naira to dollar how much is it?

Naija News reports that in the black market, the players buy a dollar for N560 and sell for N565 on Tuesday morning, December 7, 2021, after they bought N560 and sold for N565 on Monday morning, December 6.

Meanwhile, Naija News reports that the USD started this week at ₦565 in the parallel market also known as the black market on Monday, December 6, 2021. A change of N7 compared to last Monday, November 29, 2021, when it opened at ₦558 per dollar.
