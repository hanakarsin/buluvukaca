Do nurses go for NYSC?

You can join the National Youth Service Corps program after completing your nursing degree or ND/HND. However, you cannot serve NYSC if you do Registered Nursing (RN). Registered Nurses or people who go for Nursing school programs (RN) are not allowed to serve at NYSC because of these reasons:

  • The BSN or degree in Nursing and ND/HND takes five years, while a nursing program in a college takes three years.
  • College nursing graduates receive the Registered Nursing (RN) certificate. Meanwhile, university nursing graduates get RN, Registered Midwifery (RM), and BSN certificates, and nursing graduates with ND/HND have RN, RM, and HND.
  • Students from schools under JAMB can join NYSC because they can provide a JAMB registration number during NYSC admission. Schools/Colleges of Nursing are not under JAMB.
