Meredith Baxter - Age, Family, Bio

Portrayed television mom Elyse Keaton on Family Ties with Michael J. Fox. She also acted in Dan Vs. from 2011 to 2013.

Before Fame

She debuted on television in a short-lived comedy Bridget Loves Bernie.

Trivia

She is only fourteen years older than Michael J. Fox, who played her son in Family Ties.

Family Life

She married and divorced twice before doing it again with Michael Blodgett in 1995.

Associated With

She starred in Family Ties with Michael Gross.
