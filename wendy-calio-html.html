Wendy Calio - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and dancer who became famous for playing the role of Nina on a Disney Junior television series called Imagination Movers.

Before Fame

She began studying ballet and jazz during her childhood years and made her first television appearance at the age of twelve.

Trivia

She performed in the 2007 national tour of High School Musical.

Family Life

She was born in Hawaii to a family of Filipino descent. She married Jeremy Gilbert in 2008.

Associated With

She appeared in 1998 in Cosmo's Tale, a film directed by Adam Shankman.
