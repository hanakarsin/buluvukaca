Ryan Trahan - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber most notable for his comedy and lifestyle content. His knowledge and expertise has brought more than 8 million subscribers to his self-titled channel so far. He initially gained fame for his videos sharing his background as a runner. 

Before Fame

He lived in Eagle Lake, Texas before attending Texas A&M University. After complications with the NCAA, he decided to drop out of college to pursue entrepreneurship full-time. 

Trivia

He's the co-founder of Neptune Bottle and Hydra Collective.  He went viral in 2021 after making a YouTube video to show how he survived on 1 penny for an entire week.

Family Life

He has a brother named Matthew. He began dating Haley Pham in late 2018 and the two got married in November 2020. He and Haley ran the YouTube channel The Traphamily together. 

Associated With

He and Breeja Larson have both run for the Texas A&M Aggies.
