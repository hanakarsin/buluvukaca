Ways to check Stanbic IBTC pension account balance

Stanbic IBTC pension account is a convenient way to see all the information related to your pension funds, a customer can open the account in an easy way, just download the form from official website, fill it and submit. See also: Top 10 pension companies in Nigeria.

Another convenient way to manage the account is to use Stanbic IBTC pension app. This mobile application can be installed on any smartphone, with the help of the application any customer can:

  • Check account balance
  • See last three transactions
  • Generate email statement
  • Update personal information
  • Reset or change forgotten password

It is also possible to check your pension balance by dialing *909# on your phone. Choose option 1 to see the balance. You can also send SMS to 30388 with a request for the balance.
