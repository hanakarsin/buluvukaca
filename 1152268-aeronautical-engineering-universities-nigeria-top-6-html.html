Aeronautical engineering universities in Nigeria - Top 6

Aeronautical engineering is one of the most prestigious careers globally. Qualified aeronautical engineers design and develop aircraft and spacecraft machines. The field is highly competitive, and many companies offer impressive compensation packages. If you want to join this field, you should familiarise yourself with the top aeronautical engineering universities in Nigeria.

Aeronautical engineers must excel in applied mathematics, theory, knowledge and problem-solving. In present-day Nigeria, multiple institutions of higher education offer this course.

Top 10 aeronautical engineering universities in Nigeria

Below is a list of the 10 best aeronautical engineering universities and colleges in Nigeria and their locations.

1. Nigerian College of Aviation Technology (NCAT)

  • Location: Zaria, Kaduna State
  • Year of establishment: 1964
  • Type: Public

The Nigerian College of Aviation Technology is one of the leading institutions for aviation-related courses. It was the first ever air training institute in the country. It offers various aeronautical telecommunications engineering and aircraft maintenance engineering courses.

2. Obafemi Awolowo University - Aviation Training Institute

  • Location: Ile-Ife
  • Year of establishment: 1960
  • Type: Public
  • Motto: For Learning and Culture

The Obafemi Awolowo University is one of the few federal universities that offer aeronautical engineering in Nigeria. It is one of the three institutions of higher learning established in the country between 1961 and 1962.

It was created following a report submitted to the Nigerian Federal Government. The institution has numerous faculties and centres of excellence, including the Aviation Training Institute.

3. Afe Babalola University

  • Location: Ado-Ekiti, Ekiti State
  • Year of establishment: 2009
  • Type: Private
  • Motto: Labor Servitum Et Integritas (Labor for service and Integrity)

Afe Babalola University is one of the few private universities offering aeronautical engineering in Nigeria. This private university was founded by lawyer Afe Babalola in 2009. The institution's College of Engineering offers aerospace and astronautical programmes.

4. Air Force Institute of Technology (Nigeria Air Force University)

  • Location: Kaduna
  • Year of establishment: 1977
  • Type: Public
  • Motto: Quest for Excellence

Air Force Institute of Technology plays a crucial role in the Nigerian Air Force. It trains people to design, handle, and maintain aircraft and spacecraft needs of the Air Force. The institution has a department of Aeronautics & Astronautics.

5. Lagos State University (LASU)

  • Location: Ojo, Lagos State
  • Year of establishment: 1983
  • Type: Public
  • Motto: Per la verità e di servizio (For Truth and Service)

LASU is an institution of higher learning that seeks excellence in teaching, research and service to humanity. Its Faculty of Engineering has an Aerospace Engineering Department. This department has qualified and experienced staff who produce competent graduates.

6. Kwara State University (KWASU)

  • Location: Malete
  • Year of establishment: 2009
  • Type: Public
  • Motto: University for community development and entrepreneurship

KWASU is one of the top universities that offer aeronautical engineering in Nigeria. It was the first Nigerian institution to establish a degree programme in aeronautical and astronautical engineering. This was announced in February 2017.

In 2021, two students recorded a significant research breakthrough. They designed and constructed a light aircraft which underwent a test flight in the first quarter of 2022.

Aeronautical engineering fees

The cost of pursuing this programme depends on the institution. Once you gain admission into one of the institutions of higher learning listed above, you will receive a fee structure showing the amount you should pay per semester.

Aeronautical engineering salary in Nigeria

The compensation packages for aeronautical engineers in the country are quite competitive. The monthly salary varies depending on the employer.
