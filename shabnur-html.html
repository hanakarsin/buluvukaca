Jul 3, 2024 Lourie Helzer

Shabnur - Age, Family, Bio

Leading Bengalishi actress who won a 2006 Best Actress Award from the National Film Awards for her role in Dui Noyoner Alo.

Before Fame

She made her acting debut at age 20 in the 1984 film Tomakey Chai.

Trivia

She starred as Pa Kajol in the top-grossing national film of 2007, Amar Praner Swami.

Family Life

She and her siblings were raised in Dhaka, Bangladesh. In 2012 she married Anik Mahmud and the couple later welcomed a son named Aijan.

Associated With

She co-starred in the 2007 film Amar Praner Swami with leading man Shakib Khan.
