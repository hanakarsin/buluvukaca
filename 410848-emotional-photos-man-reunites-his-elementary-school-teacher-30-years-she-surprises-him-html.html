Emotional Photos as Man Reunites with His Elementary School Teacher after 30 Years, She Surprises Hi

- A man has reunited with his elementary school teacher after more than 25 years

- The man identified as Nicholas Ferroni got many people emotional as he shared photos from their meeting

- To his amazement, the female teacher did something that left him surprised

A man has left many heart-warmed as he linked up with his kindergarten teacher after 30 years. The overjoyed man identified as Nicholas Ferroni took to Twitter to share the news.

Ferroni, while showering encomiums on the teacher, recalled how she always told him that he was such a great artist.

He added that she meant everything to him.

Describing what transpired during their reunion, Ferroni - who is now a teacher - said they both cried upon seeing each other.

However, his surprise meeting with her was rewarded with a surprise from her.
