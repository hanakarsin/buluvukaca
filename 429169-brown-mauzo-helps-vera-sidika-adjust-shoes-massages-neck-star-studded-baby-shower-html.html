Brown Mauzo Helps Vera Sidika Adjust Shoes, Massages Her Neck During Star-Studded Baby Shower

  • Vera and Mauzo hooked up in 2020 and in August 2021, they celebrated their first dating anniversary with sweet messages
  • Their star-studded baby shower was attended by both men and women
  • While addressing their guests, the singer surprised his lover with a parcel of land but it is not clear where it is within the city or on the outskirts
  • Vera's baby shower was held at her friend Kevin Obia's place and she pampered him with praises, disclosing that they have been friends for 12 years

Singer Brown Mauzo and his current lover Vera Sidika are expecting their first child.

The lovebirds hooked up in August 2020 and moved in together. In August 2021, Vera and Mauzo celebrated their first dating anniversary with sweet messages.

On Sunday, October 2021, Vera held her extravagant baby shower at businessman Kevin Obia's posh Nairobi residence.

The event was attended by the socialite's close friends and a few family members. During the baby shower, Mauzo at some point massaged Vera's back and neck and even helped her adjust her shoes.

The coastal based singer looked sharp in a white suit and a pink pocket square while the heavily pregnant Vera dressed in a long pink custom-made dress.

While addressing their guests, the singer surprised his lover with a parcel of land but it is not clear where it is within the city or on the outskirts.

Why Vera planned her own baby shower

The heavily pregnant socialite revealed that she planned her own baby shower because she didn't want disappointments.

"Hope y'all now know why I would prefer to plan my own baby shower and not wait to be surprised by anyone. I am a perfectionist. Nobody can see my vision but me. Told y'all baby shower would be better," she said.

Baby shower venue

The famous socialite praised her best friend Kevin Obia for offering her his place to hold her baby shower.

"Getting a nice place garden with beautiful trees in the background for a small party of 40 guests wasn't easy," she wrote.

She showered Obia with praises, disclosing that they have been friends for 12 years.

"He was kind enough to welcome us and our guests to his beautiful residence. his garden was the perfect venue anyone could ever ask for and I wanted nothing less."

Vera Sidika talks about conceiving

In a related article published on TUKO.co.ke, Vera claimed that she conceived on Valentine's Day, February 14, 2021.

Vera disclosed that she removed the contraceptive she was using in January 2021, and a few weeks, she got pregnant.

"I conceived on February 14, Valentine's Day. I know this because that's the date I ovulated," she said.
