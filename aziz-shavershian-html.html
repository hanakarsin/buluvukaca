Aziz Shavershian - Trivia, Family, Bio

Australian bodybuilder and model who established a following after posting videos of himself on YouTube using the name "Zyzz." He passed away from a congenital heart defect in Thailand on August 5, 2011.

Before Fame

He was inspired to become a bodybuilder by his older brother, and he began to pursue bodybuilding after he graduated from secondary school.

Trivia

He published Zyzz's Bodybuilding Bible in May of 2011 and launched his own protein label, Protein of the Gods, in June of 2011.

Family Life

He was raised by his parents, Maiane Iboian and Sergei Shavershian, and had a brother, Said Shavershian.

Associated With

Arnold Schwarzenegger was one of his favorite bodybuilders.
