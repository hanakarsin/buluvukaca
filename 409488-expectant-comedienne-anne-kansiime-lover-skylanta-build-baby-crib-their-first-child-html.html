Expectant Comedienne Anne Kansiime, Lover Skylanta Build Baby Crib for Their First Child

- Kansiime said they used three days to make their first-ever baby's crib

- The comedienne disclosed that it was quite fulfilling building the baby crib on their own

- TUKO.co.ke had earlier reported that Kansiime and Skylanta are expecting their first baby together

Top Ugandan comedienne Anne Kansiime and her lover Skylanta must be the happiest people in the world right now.

TUKO.co.ke earlier reported that comedienne Anne Kansiime had come out to announce that she was expectant with her first baby.

The news excited the comedian and her lover Skylanta so much that they could not keep to themselves anymore.

Days after announcing they are expecting, the couple gave their fans another reason to love them even more.

How, you may ask? Being celebrities, Kansiime and her man can hire anyone to do anything for them, including making several things in readiness for their baby.

TUKO.co.ke has learnt the couple made their own baby court and were excited about it, judging by Kansiime's social media post.

The celebrated comedienne shared a photo of her guy Skylanta painting their almost complete baby crib on her IG and disclosed how satisfying it was making it.

The star noted that it took them three days to fix the baby court, but it was a very joyous moment for them.

''We decided to build our first crib ourselves and I must say it was a very good satisfying three days," Kansiime captioned.

Her post excited many people who have been following them.

Many of their fans and followers were impressed and camped on their respective social media platforms to laud them for being good parents even way before welcoming their baby.
