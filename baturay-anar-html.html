Baturay Anar - Age, Family, Bio

Turkish YouTube star and gamer best recognized as the creator of his channel NDNG - Baturay. He has gained more than 3 million subscribers creating Let's Play videos of games like Grand Theft Auto, Counter Strike, and various virtual reality games.

Before Fame

In December of 2014, he opened his YouTube channel with the video "Türkçe Five Nights At Freddy's 2 - Oç Baloncu - Bölüm 1 w/Facecam."

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos is "KUSUCAM!! - Ingrown Toenail Simulator" which earned more than 2 million views after it was posted in May 2015.

Family Life

He was born and raised in Istanbul, Turkey.

Associated With

He is friends with fellow YouTube gamer and vlogger Enes Batur.
