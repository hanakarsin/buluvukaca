Alana Kern - Age, Family, Bio

Social media influencer and Instagram model who rose to fame by sharing fashion, swimwear, and lifestyle photos on her alana_kern_ account. She frequently posts updates from her scenic travels around the world. She has more than 310,000 followers on the platform.

Before Fame

She made her Instagram debut in late 2019. One of her earliest photos was taken at the Wailea Beach Resort - Marriott, Maui.

Trivia

As an influencer she has modeled and promoted the active and swimwear brand teambeijogirls. She is also an actor and singer. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Hawaii, but now lives in Utah.

Associated With

She posed for a photo with TV actor DeLijah McAlpin in early 2021.
