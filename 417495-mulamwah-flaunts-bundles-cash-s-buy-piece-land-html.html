Mulamwah Flaunts Bundles of Cash, Says He's Going to Buy His First Piece of Land

  • Mulamwah said it has not been easy but thanked God for enabling him
  • He noted the money he was going to use to buy a piece of land all came from his comedy career
  • The comedian shared a video of himself holding wards of new notes

Comedian Mulamwah has come out to encourage and inspire young men with his success story.

Mulamwah on Thursday, July 1, shared a video of himself holding wards of notes and disclosed he was going to buy his first-ever piece of land.

The comedian noted that he got all the money from comedy as he encouraged young comedians not to give up.

According to Mulamwah, it has not always been smooth sailing and he worked so hard to achieve this important milestone.

In his caption, the comedian urged young people to always think of investing whenever they get good money.

''God is great!! Today I am going to buying my first ka shamba. It is all possible, no matter what, just believe, pray and put in work.To more wins.Foot on pedal.Youth: Investment is key. Sherehe baadae . KONKI !!'' Mulamwa wrote.

Mulamwah noted that he is planning to set up a house on the piece of land.

His post inspired many of his followers who camped on the comments section to congratulate him.

The latest development comes about a year after the comedian threw in the towel and abandoned his comedy career briefly.

The actor who always had a comical take on normal life occurrences burnt his signature jacket to express his sadness over his departure.

In a saddening Twitter post, the entertainer said his decision was informed by varied valid reasons including the loss of his baby.

The heartbroken guy who rose to fame thanks to Instagram skits said his life was filled with constant attacks, rebuke and trolling ever since he joined the entertainment industry.

Mulamwah said he was human and has had to stomach a lot of bile from naysayers who did not consider his feelings or those of his girlfriend.

According to the public figure, he expected his career to bring smiles to people’s faces and lift the spirits of those who were sad and down.

However, instead of being fulfilled, he has had to deal with self-doubt, disappointment and unsolicited criticism from people who did not know better.

He, however, made a comeback after he was consoled and encouraged by fellow entertainers such as Jalang'o who asked him to sail on and don't mind naysayers.
