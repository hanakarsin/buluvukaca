Remains Of Ibrahim Attahiru, Others Arrive Abuja For Burial

The remains of the Chief of Army Staff, Lt Gen Ibrahim Attahiru and others who died in a Beachcraft 350 aircraft that crashed at the Kaduna International Airport on Friday have arrived in Abuja.

The remains of the military officers were received at the Presidential Wing of the Nnamdi Azikwe International Airport, Abuja about 11am.

They were moved to the National Mosque, Abuja and the Protestant Church at the Air Force base, Abuja.
