Savannah James - Age, Family, Bio

Best known as the wife of NBA superstar LeBron James. She has over 1.8 million followers on her mrs_savannahrj Instagram account, where she frequently posts pictures of her family. 

Before Fame

She and LeBron James began dating when they were 16.

Trivia

In 2010, she and LeBron James released their own furniture line called Home Court by LeBron James.

Family Life

Her maiden name is Brinson. She and LeBron James married in 2013. She had a son named LeBron Jr. in October of 2004, followed by a second son named Bryce Maximus in June 2007 and a daughter named Zhuri in October 2014.

Associated With

She watched her husband win two NBA Championships playing alongside Dwyane Wade on the Miami Heat.
