Jacoby Shaddix - Age, Family, Bio

Rock singer best known as the front man for the alternative rock band Papa Roach, best known for the hit song "Last Resort." Their album Infest helped propel them to global stardom in the early 2000's.

Before Fame

He worked as a dish washer and janitor in a hospital. He began pursuing music seriously in 1999.

Trivia

He was the host for the show, Scarred, on MTV. He notoriously cited a riot at Ozzfest in the year 2000, much to the dismay of Sharon Osbourne. 

Family Life

He married Kelly Shaddix in 1997. They have three sons together.

Associated With

He was featured on Carlos Santana's song, "Smoke on the Water."
