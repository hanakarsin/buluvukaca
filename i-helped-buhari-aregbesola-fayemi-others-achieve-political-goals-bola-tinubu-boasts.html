I Helped Buhari, Aregbesola, Fayemi, Others Achieve Political Goals

A presidential aspirant and national leader of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Ahmed Tinubu has revealed that he helped a lot of people achieve their political goals.

The former Lagos State governor made this known when a paid a visit to the traditional rulers in the three senatorial district of Ondo State.

Speaking at the palace of the Deji of Akure, Tinubu said he was in the state to seek the monarch’s support for his presidential aspiration.

According to him, he helped Muhammadu Buhari to become president.

Among the beneficiaries of his political benevolence, Tinubu also mentioned a former governor of Osun and Ondo States, Ogbeni Rauf Aregbesola, Dr Olusegun Mimiko respectively.

He also mentioned Ekiti State Governor, Dr Kayode Fayemi, among others .

Tinubu said, “I helped Mimiko got his mandate through court after two years that he was elected. I helped Fayemi get his mandate after three years. It took Aregbesola three years, 10 months, to get his mandate. We have helped many people like that.”

Meanwhile, Tinubu has revealed that he is the best candidate for the 2023 presidency and he would not give any excuse for failure.

He stated that he would drop the baton after spending eight years in power. He went ahead to list some of the things he plans to do when he gets into power.
