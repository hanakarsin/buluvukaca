Johnnie Ray - Trivia, Family, Bio

1950s rock 'n' roll singer who recorded "Cry" and "The Little White Cloud That Cried," which sold more than two million copies.

Before Fame

He lost hearing in his right ear after a Boy Scout blanket-toss mishap.

Trivia

He released more than fifty hit singles over the course of his career.

Family Life

He married Marilyn Morrison on May 25, 1952 and they divorced in January 1954.

Associated With

He sang "Candy Lips" with Doris Day.
