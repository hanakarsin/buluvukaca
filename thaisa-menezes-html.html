Thasa Menezes - Age, Family, Bio

Middle-blocker and two-time Olympic gold medalist who has played for the Brazil women's national volleyball team. She won back-to-back gold medals at the Olympics in 2008 and 2012. She has won numerous Best Spiker and Best Blocker Awards.

Before Fame

She started playing club volleyball with Minas Tenis Club in 2002. She joined the national team in 2005.

Trivia

She was awarded the Best Blocker Award for the 2011 Pan American Cup, as well as gold for her national team.

Family Life

She is of Lebanese and Brazilian descent. She was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Associated With

She and teammate Dani Lins have both won gold medals for the Brazil national team in the Olympics.
