List of elected candidates for 2019 House of Representatives election

The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) has released a list of the members of the House of Representatives elected in the February 23 elections.

The All Progressives Congress (APC) retains the majority with 211 seats. The lead opposition Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) has 111 seats.

The All Progressives Grand Alliance (APGA) got six seats, the Action Democratic Congress (ADC) got three seats, Action Alliance (AA) won two and the Peoples Redemption Party (PRP) also got two seats.
