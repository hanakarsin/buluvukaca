'My Daughter Has Achieved So Much In 30 Years'

Nigerian billionaire, Femi Otedola, has bragged about his daughter’s achievements on the occasion of her 30th birthday today.

Naija News reports that the popular female disc jockey, Florence Ifeoluwa Otedola, took to her Instagram page to share lovely photos to celebrate a new milestone.

She also penned a tribute to herself, expressing appreciation to God, noting she had decided not to throw any lavish birthday but celebrate the new age in prayers and with family members.

According to Cuppy, she is applauding herself for the first time because she is proud of everything she has accomplished.

She wrote: “Yes, Lord, you did it again, you gave me another year. Happy 30th Birthday to me! Earlier on this year, I decided that I wouldn’t do some silly lavish cuppy party but instead enter my new age in prayer and surrounded by family only.

“For once, I’m going to CLAP for myself and say- I am SO PROUD of ALL I’ve accomplished, and SO EXCITED for the JOY my 30’s will bring. Happy birthday, incredible Cuppy, ME!”

You Have Achieved So Much

On his part, Femi Otedola took to his Instagram page to celebrate DJ Cuppy, stating he is proud of the woman she has become.

He noted that his daughter has achieved so much over the years and prayed that God will bless her next thirty years.

He wrote: “Happy 30th Birthday to my pink-haired angel, Papa is so proud of the woman you have become. You have achieved so much in these 30 years and May God Bless your next 30. Love You …F.Ote”
