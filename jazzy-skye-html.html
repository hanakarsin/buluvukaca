Jazzy Skye - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube personality and content creator who is recognized for being a member of Kids Fun TV's Fun Squad. Her videos consist of Ninja Warrior style competitions, gymnastics, DIY arts and crafts, and toy demonstrations. She has amassed more than 700,000 subscribers.

Before Fame

Her oldest archived video, "Mystery Doll Spies on Jazzy in Real Life! Is It the Crazy Doll from the DollMaker?," was posted in November 2019.

Trivia

One of her most popular videos is a vlog about getting braces. The video has been viewed in excess of 7.5 million times. She wrote a song for Payton called "Best Friends Forever" and posted a music video for it in December of 2020. 

Family Life

She has a twin brother named Jack Skye. Her other siblings are Kade, Kalia and Kyler. 

Associated With

She's been best friends with Payton and Paxton Myler.
