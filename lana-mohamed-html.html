Lana Mohamed - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star who gained popularity for posting dance and lip sync videos on her lana_mohd89 TikTok account. Her September 11, 2022, clip of her getting ready to go to the Rock in Rio festival received over 10,000 views. She occasionally posts videos of herself playing guitar and singing. 

Before Fame

She posted her first TikTok video on July 25, 2016, which was a lip sync video featuring herself and a teddy bear. 

Trivia

She and her sister Leen Mohd co-star on their Super Sisters YouTube channel, where they often post vlogs and challenge videos. Their YouTube channel has accumulated more than 4.5 million subscribers. Her lana_mohd89 TikTok account has gained 10 million followers and 181 million likes. 

Family Life

She lives with her family in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. She and her sister Leen have a brother named Mustafa. 

Associated With

In July 2022, she posted a TikTok set to Russ Millions and Tion Wayne's song "Body."
