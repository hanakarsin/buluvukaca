Chauncey Koziol

DGR - Age, Family, Bio

Also known as DanGoodRepairs, he is a YouTube gamer well known for publishing speedruns from Super Mario games such as Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Odyssey. 

Before Fame

He initially signed up for YouTube to upload video game repair videos. 

Trivia

In rare cases, he would post gameplay from other games such as Legend of Zelda and Mega Man. 

Family Life

His first name is Dave. His wife Amanda has taken over his channel for a number of videos. 

Associated With

He and Cheese05 have both been known for their Mario speedruns.
