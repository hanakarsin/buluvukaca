Journey Christine - Age, Family, Bio

Television actress who is best known for her role as Zola in the series Hidden Springs. She has also appeared as Maya in the television series The Upshaws. She has appeared in more than 30 episodes of television throughout her developing career. 

Before Fame

Her first professional acting role was in a Kohl's commercial when she was just 13 months old. 

Trivia

She has been featured in commercials for Walmart, Honda, Disney, and Home Depot. In 2021, she played Gabbi in the movie Outsiders. 

Family Life

She was born in Los Angeles, California. She is one of four siblings and is both African-American and Samoan. 

Associated With

She and actress Kiernan Shipka have both starred in the Netflix series Let It Snow.
