Late Mercy Waciama's Children, Husband Release Emotional Song as Tribute to Bishop

- The late bishop Waciama left behind three children and her husband

- The family released a song to express their pain and to ask God to take it away

- The woman of God succumbed to COVID-19 complications on March 16

The late bishop Mercy Waciama's husband and children have released a song in her honour a few weeks after they laid the bishop to rest.

Waciama succumbed to COVID-19 complications on Tuesday, March 16, while receiving treatment at a Murang'a based hospital.

Her family poured out their hearts in their new song Ruo Ruru (This Pain) in which they expressed their hurt and asked God to help them overcome it according to His will.

"This pain is too much Father (God) and our hearts are broken, May you take it away and bless us," the chorus of the emotional song says.

In the video, clips from Waciama's burial are seen. Nonetheless, her three daughters and husband seem strong and it is without a doubt that they will overcome this.

TUKO.co.ke reported that the late bishop always kept her family under the scathing eye of the public, photos showed she adored her family with every fibre of her being.

The deceased bishop left behind her husband identified as Mwangi Samuel Muturi and three beautiful children.

Kenyans took to various social media platforms to mourn Waciama whom many described as a strong woman of God.

Thika Town MP Patrick Wainaina eulogised Waciama as a great supportive friend and a determined servant of God who stretched far to preach and aid the lowly hearted.

"It is with profound sorrow that I have received the news of the passing on of my family friend Bishop Mercy Waciama of Kiheo TV. May God give her family strength and courage to sail on," he said.

Just before her death, the preacher was asking the faithful to assist her ongoing construction of Embracing God Church in Thika, Kiambu county.

She was a host of Ugi Ni Umenyo TV show at Njata TV before she went ahead to launch her own station, Kiheo TV.
