Done Deal Transfer On Deadline Day

Football clubs across Europe major leagues have signed one or two players on the deadline of the summer transfer window which closed on August 31.

English Premier League side, Manchester United on Tuesday signed former Juventus player, Cristiano Ronaldo for £19.8m.

The Red Devils also sold Daniel James to Leeds for £25m.

North London side, Arsenal on Tuesday also signed Takehiro Tomiyasu from Bologna for £19.7m.
