Ryder Tully - Age, Family, Bio

Pop music performer who is known for songs such as "Good Feelings" and "Number one." He has more than 180,000 followers on his rydertullymusic Instagram account where he promotes his art. His single "It's Alright" has become one of his most popular songs on Spotify. 

Before Fame

He began his career by posting lip sync videos in 2021.

Trivia

He is a member of the ROCK SQUAD YouTube channel. He is also affiliated with the Rock Your Hair concert series. 

Family Life

His social media accounts are managed by his parents. He has a sister named Lyla. He began a relationship with Peja Anne in 2022 but they broke up in May 2023. 

Associated With

Both he and Nidal Wonder are members of the ROCK SQUAD channel.
