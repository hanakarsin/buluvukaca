Stradman - Age, Family, Bio

Supercar enthusiast who earned more than 10 million views for the video "Opening up the Pagani Huayra ft. the $2.5Million Ferrari F60 America." His automotive channel has earned more than 837 million total views.

Before Fame

He debuted on YouTube with a video called "Ferrari Dino 246 GTS" in July of 2012.

Trivia

His screen name of "Strad" comes from his dream car; the 2004 Ferrari Challenge Stradale.

Family Life

His name is James Condon and he is from Park City, Utah. He dated Tessa Peay.

Associated With

He and Paul Wallace both run channels featuring supercars.
