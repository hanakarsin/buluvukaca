Ashley Grace - Age, Family, Bio

Mexican singer who rose to fame as one half of the country-pop duo Ha*Ash alongside her sister Hanna Nicole. The duo is best known for songs like "No Te Quiero Nada" and is the recipient of numerous awards, including the Canción Corta-Venas award at the 2016 Premios Juventud ceremony.

Before Fame

She began singing at age five and won a talent contest held by her school at age twelve.

Trivia

She appeared on a 2009 episode of the series Plaza Sésamo and contributed the song "Me Muevo Así al Caminar" to it.

Family Life

Her full name is Ashley Grace Pérez Mosa. Her father is from Mexico and her mother is from the United States.

Associated With

In 2016, she recorded the song "Mi Niña Mujer" with her sister and Los Ángeles Azules.
