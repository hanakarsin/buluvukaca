Velvet Sky - Age, Family, Bio

Female wrestler who became a TNA Women's Knockout Champion.

Before Fame

She made her wrestling debut with World Xtreme Wrestling in 2004.

Trivia

She appeared in a music video for Montgomery Gentry's song 'So Called Life.'

Family Life

Her full name is Jamie Lynn Szantyr. She participated in cheerleading and softball during high school. She dated wrestlers Gregory Helmes and later Chris Sabin. She's also dated Mark LoMonaco.

Associated With

She was featured on an episode of MTV's Made with A.J. Styles.
