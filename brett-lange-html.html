Brett Maverick Lange - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram personality turned YouTuber who would earn 1.7 million subscribers on the latter platform by posting videos about reactions, challenges, fitness, nutrition, men's fashion, lifestyle and more.

Before Fame

He worked at a steakhouse while pursuing a more full-time YouTube career. He played football in his youth and developed a love for all things motocross. He was a talented track star who set records for his school.

Trivia

He spent a semester at Università degli studi di Trieste in Italy in 2016, but would attend the University of Northern Colorado full-time. He has done some modeling.

Family Life

He has an older sister named Danielle, and older brother Justin. His father is named Bill.

Associated With

He accredits reading the book "Meditations" by Marcus Aurelius as being a turning point in his life.
