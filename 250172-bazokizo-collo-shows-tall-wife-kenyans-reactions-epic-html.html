Bazokizo Collo shows off his tall wife and Kenyans reactions are epic

-Gospel artist Collo and his wife celebrated their first year wedding anniversary just recently

-He posted a picture of his family introducing his wife who appears taller than he is

-The picture has has sparked numerous reactions on social media mostly from women who claim they cannot date shorter men

Rapper Collo and his wife just celebrated their wedding anniversary, each sending the other sweet messages of love on social media.

Taking to social media, Collo’s wife Ida jotted a sweet message for him as they turned a year older in their marriage life.

Ida, gushed over her hubby, and thanked him for being her true love for the eight years they’ve dated and for the one year they have been married.
