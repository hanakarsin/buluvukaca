Eric Montanez - Age, Family, Bio

Actor, model and social star who became popular on TikTok, earning more than 3.4 million fans to his ericmontanez account by posting a mix of lip-sync and comedy videos. He was cast on the Brat series Attaway General. 

Before Fame

He began to make a name for himself on TikTok in 2019.

Trivia

He signed as a model with Vertex Management Group in 2019 and earned a clothing deal with Elwood. He earned north of 400,000 likes for one TikTok video called "Don't tell mom."

Family Life

He is from San Diego, California.

Associated With

He used an original sound called "wholesome twist" by Adam Dillon for a TikTok video captioned as "always makes my day."
