Families of Caroline Kangogo, Murdered Cop John Ogweno Bump into Each Other in Nakuru

  • The families of Caroline Kangogo and police constable John Ogweno met on Thursday, July 22
  • Kangogo, who was found dead on Friday, July 16, was linked to the murder of Ogweno in Nakuru
  • Kangogo's father passed his condolences to Ogweno's kin and also apologised on behalf of his family for his death

The families of corporal Caroline Kangogo and murdered police constable John Ogweno bumped into each other in Nakuru earlier today, Thursday, July 22.

Kangogo, who until her death in her parents' bathroom on Friday, July 16, was wanted for the murder of Ogweno and a Juja businessman Peter Ndwiga.

According to a report by Daily Nation, the unplanned meeting happened at the Kasarani police quarters where the two officers lived together before their deaths.

Kangogo's father, Barnabas Korir officially gave his condolences to Ogweno's uncle David Gombai and also apologised on behalf of his family for his daughter's alleged murder of the police constable.

"He came to see us and apologised on behalf of his family for his daughter’s alleged actions and for the loss of our son. It was just a brief meeting since we were in a hurry,” Gombai said.

Kangogo's family was at the quarters to pick their daughter's belongings when they found Ogweno's prayer service taking place.

After the prayer service, Ogweno's family said an autopsy report revealed that he died from a single gunshot to the head.

He is survived by two widows, one in Kericho and another in Homa Bay county.

The constable will be buried tomorrow, Friday, July 23, at Segre Village in Karachuony sub-county.

Ogweno's death

As TUKO.co.ke reported, Ogweno’s lifeless body was discovered inside the parking yard in Kasarani police station, on Monday, July 5, when his colleague, sergeant Joseph Ologe was taking a walk around the area.

A Police OB report indicated that the police constable was shot on the right side of his head and bled to death. A Ceska pistol assigned to the deceased was discovered missing.

She became a fugitive for 10 days before she reportedly took her own life on Friday, July 16.
