Do Jax Taylor and Brittany Cartwright Have Kids? Let's Meet Their Son

Jax and Brittany are returning to the small screen on 'The Valley.' Fans will get a glimpse into their lives as they raise their son.

By Alizabeth Swain

Mar. 20 2024, Published 10:16 p.m. ET

In the ever-evolving journey of parenthood, few stories capture the hearts of fans quite like that of Jax Taylor and Brittany Cartwright. Fans want to know if the couple has kids. Since welcoming their son Cruz in April 2021, this beloved couple from Vanderpump Rules fame has openly shared the milestones, challenges, and sheer joy of raising their child in the public eye.

Jax and Brittany have been very open about the dynamics of celebrity parenting, showcasing the blend of ordinary family challenges with the extraordinary circumstances of raising a child in the limelight. As they share snippets of their life, from joyful celebrations to everyday parenting hurdles, Jax and Brittany's story offers a candid look into the realities of raising a child while navigating fame.

Do Jax and Brittany have kids? They are proud parents to one child.

In the spring of 2021, former Vanderpump Rules stars Jax Taylor and Brittany Cartwright embarked on one of life's most profound journeys — parenthood. Welcoming their son, Cruz Michael Cauchi, on April 12, 2021, in Los Angeles marked a significant milestone in their lives, both personally and publicly. The couple's excitement and anticipation for their new roles as parents were palpable, reflecting a deep, shared commitment to their growing family.

The couple's openness about their 27-hour labor, shared in detail with E! News, not only highlighted the challenges they faced during the birth process but also the profound meaning behind Cruz's name. Their honesty provided a deeper insight into their personal lives, showcasing their resilience and the depth of their love for their newborn son.

Jax and Brittany have signed up to share their personal lives on 'The Valley.'

Jax and Brittany have made a notable return to the small screen with the launch of their new Bravo series, The Valley, according to Deadline. Premiering in the spring of 2024, the series promises an intimate look into their lives post-Vanderpump Rules, focusing on their adventures in suburban living alongside their son, Cruz.

According to the Hollywood Reporter, Brittany revealed that she and Jax are currently "taking time apart." This news came just weeks before the premiere of The Valley, adding an unexpected twist to their narrative. Despite this separation, the couple has expressed that their commitment to co-parenting Cruz remains unwavering, highlighting the complexities of navigating relationships in the public eye.

In a candid discussion about the challenges they face with Bravo TV, Taylor mentioned that besides their son, a shared business venture has been a significant factor in keeping them connected. This element of their relationship introduces a dynamic aspect to The Valley, as it showcases how they manage to maintain professional and personal ties amid personal upheavals.
