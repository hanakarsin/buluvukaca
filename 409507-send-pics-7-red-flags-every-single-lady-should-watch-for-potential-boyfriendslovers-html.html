Well, below are seven red flags every single lady should watch out for in a potential boyfriend.

1. Asks for money

If a man asks you for money, once, twice, or ten times, run my sister! He will make it a habit of borrowing money from you and never refund. Let him work hard and get his own.

2. Calls you nonstop

This is a red flag you should never ignore. He is either insecure or very idle. Communication is key to a healthy relationship but overdoing it is a clear indication that it won't last. If he keeps calling every minute in the name of checking up on you, think twice before taking things to another level.

3. Keeps giving false promises

If your prince charming is a serial liar, that is a red flag you shouldn't ignore. If he keeps promising you good things but doesn't fulfil any of them, don't waste your time, dear, move on.

4. Is a mama's boy

Men attached to their mothers find it hard to trust other women. Also, such men will include their mothers in each decision they make, and you'll have no say in that relationship. His mother will control it.

5. I need a favour type of man

If his favours are all about you borrowing money from Fuliza (mobile money app) for him, dump that man, dear sister. In the end, he will leave you in debts, and you'll struggle alone to repay. Never do anything to impress a man who can walk out of your life any time.

If he has money loan apps on his phones, run sister, he might be a serial loan defaulter, and if you end up with such a man, you will never be happy.

6. Spends the whole day at the gym instead of going to work

Fitness is part of a healthy lifestyle, but if your man is always at the gym drooling over women and not thinking of working so that you can build your future together, you're in trouble. He isn't the type of man you should waste your time with.

7. Send me photos

If your man keeps asking you for photos, especially if you're in the same vicinity, he's idle. Why can't he visit you and see you face to face? If he asks for explicit pics every time you chat, that's a clear indication that he's after physical attention and not love. A man who respects you can't be begging for photos from you.
