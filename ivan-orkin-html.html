Ivan Orkin - Age, Family, Bio

Also known as Ivan Ramen, he is a professional chef known for specializing in Ramen based dishes. He owns and operates two restaurants in Japan as well as the Ivan Ramen Slurp Shops at the Gotham West Market on 11th Avenue in New York and on the Lower East side. He is also known for having been featured in an episode of the Netflix series Chef's Table. 

Before Fame

His career in the restaurant industry started when he was fifteen and worked as a dishwasher at a sushi bar. He then attended the University of Colorado, Boulder where he studied Japanese. He left to teach abroad and returned in 1990 to attend the Culinary Institute of America. 

Trivia

He is also known for having published his own cookbook entitled "Ivan Ramen". 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Long Island, New York. The original idea to open up a Ramen style shop in Japan was a suggestion from his wife. 

Associated With

Other professional chefs who have also been featured and profiled in the Netflix series Chef's Table include Ben Shewry, Francis Mallmann, and Massimo Bottura.
