Bodhi Sabongui - Age, Family, Bio

Actor who is best known for his role as Trevor on the Netflix reboot of The Baby-Sitters Club (2020). He has also appeared in the series Legends of Tomorrow and A Million Little Things. He has acted in over 10 episodes of television since the start of his career in 2017.

Before Fame

His first acting role was in the short film Shakey's Coffee. 

Trivia

He appeared in the Netflix original film The Main Event. He had a role in the DC film Black Adam, released in 2022. 

Family Life

His parents Patrick Sabongui and Kyra Zagorsky are both actors. 

Associated With

He appears alongside Xochitl Gomez on The Baby-Sitters Club.
