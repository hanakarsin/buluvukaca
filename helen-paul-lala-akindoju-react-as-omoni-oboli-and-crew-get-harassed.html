Helen Paul, Lala Akindoju React As Omoni Oboli And Crew Get Harassed

Nollywood actress and mother of three boys Omoni Oboli has cried out over the incessant attack from area boys popularly called ‘Omo Onile’ at movie sets.

A recent video shared on her Instagram page captured Omoni Oboli and one of the touts in a heated argument as he refused to leave their movie set.

Omoni Oboli said she has taken permission from the royal corporation and the touts still want to disrupt her movie production.

The thespian who seemed displeased with the act threatened the tout to leave her movie set or else he would sleep in jail.

Omoni Oboli added that the consistent behaviour of the area boys always makes movie shooting tiring for her.

She captioned the video: “These people make shooting so tiring! When you try to come between me and my work, I get MAD!  This guy probably thought he could mess with a small woman. E shock am las las today! Me that I haven’t closed my eyes for almost 48 hrs wey body don dey pepper me already”.

Reacting to the video, her colleagues such as Helen Paul, Doyin Kukoyi, Lala Akindoju and others expressed displeasure over how some of these area boys attempt to take advantage of Nollywood stars during movie production.

lalaakindoju wrote: “Sigh. Story of all of our lives. They always try to just take advantage. So glad he couldn’t mess with you”.

doyinkukoyi wrote: “And I hope he truly slept in jail to serve as deterrent to his types”

itshelenpaul wrote: “So sad…”

pretty_gloria16 wrote: “But it’s this not robbery by trick, this is annoying I swear, so wrong”
