Lydia Kenney - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram sensation best known as Logan Paul's assistant. Her lifestyle photos have earned her more than 390,000 followers on her lydiakenney Instagram account. She has also been featured in multiple videos on Logan's YouTube channel. 

Before Fame

She became Logan Paul's assistant in August 2017. She was featured in a video titled MY NEW ASSISTANT IS EVEN SHORTER!

Trivia

She stands at only 4'9". She has frequently tagged her fashion items in her Instagram posts, including popular brands like Skims and I.AM.GIA. 

Family Life

Originally from DeKalb, Illinois, she would also live in Los Angeles, California. She has an older brother. She began dating rapper Arizona Zervas.

Associated With

She replaced Logan Paul's previous assistant, Ayla Woodruff.
