Mercedesz Henger - Age, Family, Bio

Model, actress, and reality TV personality. She's famously known for finishing third in the Italian reality show L'isola dei famosi in 2016.

Trivia

Family Life

Her mother is the actress Eva Henger and her father is the director and writer Riccardo Schicchi. She has a brother, Riccardo Jr., and a half-sister, Jennifer Caroletti.

Associated With

She appeared in the 11th season of L'isola dei famosi with Marco Carta and Simona Ventura.
