Man Accuses Davido, Manager Of Scam (Video)

A Nigerian man identified as Timothy has stirred reaction online after he accused talented singer, David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido and his manager, Asa Asika of scam.

Timothy in the video spotted online asked why the Davido Music Worldwide boss, Davido would be so wicked and heartless.

He further stated that he does not trust the record label of the award-winning singer, adding that he knows it is not the fault of Davido but of his manager, Asika.

According to Timothy, Asika was the person who collected the sum of one hundred and fifty thousand Naira for a form to apply to Davido’s record label.

Timothy added that he purchased the form with the fund that was given to him by his parents to acquire a residence at the University of Abuja.

He noted that the development has seen him chased out of his apartment, as he is currently living with a friend in school.

Timothy called on Davido to pay him back his money if he is not interested in signing him to his record label.

Netizens were however quick to point out to the young man that he might have been a victim of online scam with someone using the identity of Davido and his manager to perpetrate fraud.
