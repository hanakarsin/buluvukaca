Michael Alvarado - Age, Family, Bio

Member of the husband and wife duo called Us the Duo alongside Carissa Alvarado. They became instant sensations via the video platforms YouTube and Vine.

Before Fame

He was a solo artist initially. He met Carissa randomly before bursting onto the scene as a duo.

Trivia

He and Carissa post to YouTube on the channel MichaelAlvaradoMusic, which has earned over 2.1 million subscribers. Their Vine channel amassed more than 5 million followers. 

Family Life

He is a North Carolina native who moved to Southern California. He and Carissa welcomed a daughter in 2018. 

Associated With

One of the duo's more popular covers is of David Guetta's "Titanium."
