Puri Jagannadh - Age, Family, Bio

Director who made Pokiri in 2006. It premiered at the 7th IIFA Film Festival held in Dubai and won him a Filmfare Award for Best Director - Telugu in 2007.

Before Fame

He made his Bollywood debut with the film Shart: The Challenge in 2004.

Trivia

He directed Bbuddah... Hoga Terra Baap in 2011, which was archived in the Oscar library. He has over 650,000 followers on his purijagannadh Instagram account.

Family Life

He married Lavanya Jagannadh.

Associated With

He and Pooja Bhatt are both Bollywood directors.
