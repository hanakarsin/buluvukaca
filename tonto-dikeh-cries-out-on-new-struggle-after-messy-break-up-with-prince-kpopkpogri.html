Tonto Dikeh Cries Out On New Struggle After Messy Break Up With Prince Kpopkpogri

Popular Nollywood actress, Tonto Dikeh has cried out about struggling with severe trust issues and challenges since the messy break up with ex-lover, Prince Kpokpogri

The mother of one made this known via her Instagram story, while sharing how hard its has become to get a personal assistant and Nanny.

This comes a few months after her messy breakup with her ex-lover and Delta State politician Prince Kpokpogri.

Naija News understands the Nollywood actress cum philanthropist has been at loggerheads with Prince Kpokpogri, a former lover, whose leaked audiotape bad-mouthing the actress surfaced on social media.

In a post shared on her Instagram story, Tonto Dikeh revealed she has developed a serious trust issue because of what had happened to her in the past.

The Nollywood actress stated that she finds it difficult to hire a personal assistant and Nanny.

Tonto Dikeh (Photo credit: Instagram)

She added that she dislikes being around people or seeing them always; hence she thinks she needs a robot.

She wrote: “I need a PA and A personal Nanny for myself. The problem is I have developed a serious mad trust issues, and I dislike being around or seeing people always. I think I need a robot.”
