Michael Weatherly - Age, Family, Bio

American television actor best known to audiences as Agent DiNozzo on the hit CBS series NCIS. He got his first start in television with a minor role on The Cosby Show. In 2016, he began starring as the title character on the TV series Bull. 

Before Fame

He attended Boston College, American University and Menlo College but dropped out of school to pursue acting.

Trivia

He also had a starring role as Logan Cale on Dark Angel.

Family Life

He was married to Amelia Heinle from 1995 until 1997 and together they had a son. He married his second wife, Bojana Jankovic in 2009. They have a son and a daughter.

Associated With

He dated Jessica Alba from 2000 to 2003 while working on Dark Angel.
